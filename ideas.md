## Selected Approach: Contemporary Studio — Warm Geometric

Chosen for its balance of visual richness and approachability. The rounded corners, soft gradients, and terracotta accent system align perfectly with the "minimal, soft gradients, rounded corners" brief. The split hero and staggered service grid provide visual interest without sacrificing clarity.
